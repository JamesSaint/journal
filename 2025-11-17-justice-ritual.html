Justice - Inner Court

025-11-17.

What happened

I showed up fully in a relationship where the other person took the benefit of my presence, my labour, and my devotion without meeting me in honesty, reciprocity, or care.

How it impacted me

It left me carrying the emotional, financial, and physical cost alone, while doubting my own reality and losing trust in my own goodness.

What I am choosing now

I choose to stand in truth, to recognise my value without distortion, and to no longer tolerate relationships where my depth is met with silence, avoidance, or extraction.
