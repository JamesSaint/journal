0. The Simple Shape of Your Path

The clearest, lowest-stress, fully legal path is:

  1. Turn your UK house into passive income (rent it out).
  2. Use that income to qualify for Portugal’s D7 Passive Income Visa.
  3. Move into rented accommodation near a naturist beach such as Meco or Costa da Caparica.
  4. Convert the visa into a residency card on arrival.

1. Reality Check: What Portugal Legally Requires From You

1.1 Brexit Reality

As a UK citizen you can stay only 90 days in any 180-day period in Portugal unless you obtain a residency visa.

1.2 The D7 Visa (Why It Fits You)

Portugal’s D7 visa is for people with predictable passive income. It is widely used by retirees, landlords, and remote entrepreneurs.

  • You must show at least €870/month passive income in 2025.
  • Rental income from your UK home meets this criterion.
  • Savings help but do not replace the need for income.

2. Your House: The Key to Portugal

Option A – Rent the House (Recommended)

  • Provides stable income for D7 requirements.
  • Lowers your risk because you keep a UK base.
  • Most psychologically stabilising while your health remains variable.

Option B – Sell the House

Possible, but emotionally and financially destabilising at present. Better as a Phase Two decision, after you have successfully settled in Portugal.

3. Where to Live Nude in Portugal (Real Naturist Locations)

Praia do Meco (Sesimbra)

  • Long-established naturist culture since the 80s.
  • Official naturist zone on the southern end.
  • Quiet, wild, cliffs, pine forest.
  • Sesimbra town offers rentals €600–1,250/month.
  • 45 minutes from Lisbon.

Praia da Bela Vista (Costa da Caparica)

  • Official naturist beach, including “Beach 19”.
  • Strong LGBTQ+ and queer-friendly environment.
  • 20–25 minutes from Lisbon.
  • Flats typically around €1,000–1,500/month.

Praia do Salto (Alentejo)

  • Official naturist beach, quieter and more dramatic.
  • Good for solitude and raw landscape.

4. What You Need to Qualify for the D7 Visa

4.1 Income & Funds

  • Minimum: €870/month passive income.
  • Rental income qualifies perfectly.
  • Helpful to show additional savings (e.g., €3k–€8k).

4.2 Required Documents (Typical)

  • Passport with 6–12 months validity.
  • National Visa Application Form.
  • 2 passport photos.
  • Proof of passive income: tenancy agreement + bank statements.
  • Bank statements showing savings (3–6 months).
  • Criminal record certificate (ACRO).
  • NIF (Portuguese tax number).
  • Portuguese bank account.
  • Proof of accommodation in Portugal.
  • Health insurance valid in Portugal for at least 1 year.
  • Cover letter explaining purpose and means.

5. Full Step-by-Step Plan

PHASE 1 – Clarify Numbers and Choose Where to Live

  1. Calculate realistic rental income for your UK home.
  2. Confirm you exceed €870/month comfortably.
  3. Choose your naturist location: Meco or Costa da Caparica.

PHASE 2 – Prepare Your UK House for Rental

  1. Contact a letting agent and request full management.
  2. Get a written rental valuation.
  3. List the property for a 12-month let.
  4. Sign tenancy agreements and generate passive income.

PHASE 3 – Begin Portugal Logistics in Parallel

  1. Obtain your NIF (can be done remotely).
  2. Open a Portuguese bank account.
  3. Research rentals in Sesimbra or Costa da Caparica.
  4. Set a realistic monthly budget (€1500–2000 total).

PHASE 4 – Secure Accommodation in Portugal

  1. Contact landlords or agencies for a 6–12 month lease.
  2. Alternatively, arrange a long-stay Airbnb or Flatio booking.
  3. Obtain written confirmation or contract.

PHASE 5 – Gather All D7 Documents

Prepare all items from Section 4.2 and organise them in a simple folder.

PHASE 6 – Submit the Application

  1. Book a D7 appointment via VFS Global UK.
  2. Attend appointment with full documentation.
  3. Processing typically takes 30–60 days.

PHASE 7 – Move to Portugal and Finalize Residency

  1. Enter Portugal within your visa validity period.
  2. Move into your chosen accommodation.
  3. Attend your residency appointment (AIMA).
  4. Receive your 2-year residence permit.
  5. Register with local council and healthcare system.

PHASE 8 – Build Your Life by the Nude Beach

  • Create daily rhythm with naturist beaches (Meco or Bela Vista).
  • Let your nervous system stabilise in sun, warmth, and ocean.
  • Grow HIA consulting slowly and sustainably.
  • Integrate into naturist and LGBTQ+ friendly communities.
