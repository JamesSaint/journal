Truth Is Love

2025-10-13

Please read this slowly.

What I want to say is not to hurt you. It is because I love you enough to show you what no one else will.

I see you. I hear you. Of course you can reflect, but what I feel you really need now is not more reflection, it is integration.

You are still living a story written in your childhood, confirmed in your marriage, and carried on through the structure with the kids. You say you do not want that life, I have seen it from our first meeting, it spilled out from you to be witnessed, but by avoiding the break, you keep choosing it.

And yet you have already shown me the other side. The glimmers. Those two weeks when the kids were away were breath, freedom, aliveness. You said yourself, “I want those two weeks as our life all the time.” That was not a fantasy, that was reality, when the old story was not in the room.

But you go back. You want freedom, but you are terrified of the pain and the blame. So you retreat into the loop: victim, child, stuck. And every time you do, the old story wins.

That was then. This is now. You are awake. You cannot unsee the truth. Especially with me beside you.

I cannot choose for you. Either you step into the life you say you want, or you stay in the old one.

There is no middle ground.

I am the only one who will tell you this truth, because I will not collude with the fog. I will only stand in what is real. What you do with it is yours.

Truth is love, even when it stings. And I am offering you that love now.

You do not have to respond. I simply ask you to sit with it.

With love,
James x
