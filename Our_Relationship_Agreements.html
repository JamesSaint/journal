Our Relationship Agreements

This document outlines the needs and commitments we agree to uphold in our relationship, in service of our shared growth, connection, and fulfillment.

Our Shared Commitments

  1. Emotional presence: We commit to attuning to each other emotionally, noticing, asking, and engaging with what’s happening inside one another.
  2. Mutual prioritization: We agree to treat each other’s needs and dreams with equal importance, ensuring we both feel like partners, not afterthoughts.
  3. Commitment to shared growth: We pledge to engage actively in personal and relational work, including therapy, honest conversations, and conflict-resolution practices.
  4. Respect for chosen roles: We recognize and value each other’s contributions, whether financial, emotional, practical, or nurturing, as equal and essential.
  5. Clear financial agreements: We will create and honor explicit agreements so that both of us have financial autonomy without guilt or constant justification.
  6. Erotic and relational polarity: We agree to cultivate an erotic connection that is intentional, alive, and safe, celebrating each other’s authentic sexual expressions.
  7. Boundaries around legacy dynamics: We acknowledge and own the impact of past or ongoing relationships on our partnership, co-creating agreements to keep our connection healthy.
  8. Shared vision: We commit to consciously building our life together — where we live, how we spend time, our financial plans, and our relational agreements — rather than drifting or reacting to outside forces.
