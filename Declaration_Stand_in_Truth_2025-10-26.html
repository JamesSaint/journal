I Will Always Stand in Truth

Written by James Saint on 26 October 2025.

There is a line I cannot step back across.

I have seen too much now: the way denial dresses itself as politeness, how cruelty hides behind concern, how fear speaks through authority. I have watched people I called family shrink from truth because it exposed what they could not bear to face.

I will never be like them. I will not trade truth for belonging. I will not twist myself to fit into the smallness of their comfort.

Bravery, for me, is no longer about fighting. It is about staying clear. It is the choice to see what is real and not look away.

Whatever happens next, I stand where I can breathe: in honesty, in integrity, in peace.

James Saint
